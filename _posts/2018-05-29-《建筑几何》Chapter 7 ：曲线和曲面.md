### Curves 曲线
-  All points of a circle or a conic section lie in a plane. These curves are called **planar curves**, in contrast to **spatial curves** such as helixes.
<img src="/images/posts/AG/karl.png" height="390" width="210">  

- Points of the curve with a uniquely defined tangent are called **regular points**. All points where the tangent vector $c'(t)$ is a non-vanishing vector are **regular points**. Curve
points where $c'(t)$ is the zero vector $o$ are
singular points.
<img src="/images/posts/AG/singular.png" height="400" width="150">  
- discrete curve 离散曲线：The polygon is also called a **discrete curve**. In many practical computations, curves are actually **polygons (i.e., discrete curves)**.

### Conic sections 锥部分
- implicit representation隐式表示：$a·x^2 + b·x·y + c·y^2 + d·x + e·y + f = 0.$
- ellipse 椭圆： $b2·x2 + a2·y2 – a2·b2 = 0$;
<img src="/images/posts/AG/ellipse.png" height="200" width="150">  
<img src="/images/posts/AG/ellipse2.png" height="400" width="300">  
- hyperbola 双曲线：$b^2·x^2 – a^2·y^2 – a^2·b^2 = 0$;
<img src="/images/posts/AG/hyperbola.png" height="320" width="170">  
<img src="/images/posts/AG/hyperbola2.png" height="430" width="230">  
- parabola 抛物线：$x^2 – 2py = 0.$
